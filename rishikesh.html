Rishikesh - The Yoga Capital of the World

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Rishikesh is from September to November and February to May when the weather is pleasant, ideal for outdoor activities and spiritual retreats.

Local Cuisine

  • Aloo Puri: A popular breakfast dish of spicy potato curry served with fried bread.
  • Kachori: Deep-fried bread stuffed with lentils or potatoes.
  • Chole Bhature: Spicy chickpea curry served with fluffy fried bread.
  • Fresh Juices: Rishikesh is known for its fresh fruit juices and herbal teas.

Festivals and Events

  • International Yoga Festival: Held in March, this festival attracts yoga enthusiasts from around the world.
  • Ganga Aarti: A daily spiritual ritual performed at Triveni Ghat and Parmarth Niketan.
  • Mahashivratri: A grand celebration dedicated to Lord Shiva, marked by night-long prayers and rituals.

Travel Tips

  • Dress Modestly: Respect local customs, especially when visiting ashrams and temples.
  • Carry Cash: Many small shops and vendors do not accept cards.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water, especially during outdoor activities.
  • Book in Advance: During peak seasons, accommodations and activities can get fully booked.

Top Attractions

  • Laxman Jhula: A iconic suspension bridge over the Ganges River.
  • Ram Jhula: Another famous bridge with stunning views of the river.
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple: A sacred temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, surrounded by lush forests.
  • Triveni Ghat: A holy ghat where the Ganga Aarti is performed every evening.

Where to Stay

  • Luxury: Ananda in the Himalayas, Aloha on the Ganges.
  • Mid-Range: Ganga Kinare, Divine Ganga Cottage.
  • Budget: Zostel Rishikesh, The Hosteller Rishikesh.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1:

  • Morning: Visit Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula.
  • Afternoon: Explore the local markets and cafes.
  • Evening: Attend the Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat.

Day 2:

  • Morning: Take a yoga or meditation class at an ashram.
  • Afternoon: Visit Neelkanth Mahadev Temple.
  • Evening: Enjoy a riverside dinner.

Day 3:

  • Morning: Go for white-water rafting on the Ganges.
  • Afternoon: Relax at a beach camp or cafe.
  • Evening: Explore the Beatles Ashram.
